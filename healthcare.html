Patient safety depends on matching identity across every tool

Healthcare organizations use separate ATS, assessment platforms, credential verification vendors, and identity management tools. Candidate data doesn't match across them — so a proxy candidate who clears an assessment under a fake identity won't be flagged when different credentials appear in the BGV report. FRACIFY sits across every tool and matches identity data at every stage — zero data stored.

ATS-to-credential identity reconciliation

The name in your ATS should match the person who took the assessment, whose credentials were verified, and who shows up on Day One. FRACIFY's UAN/EPFO audit engine cross-references every candidate's employment timeline from BGV reports against assessment session identities — flagging mismatches that indicate impersonification.

  • Instant UAN-based employment history verification against government records
  • Multi-institution employment timeline correlation
  • Automated discrepancy detection for fabricated tenures
  • Real-time qualification and license validity checks
  • Zero data retention — fully DPDP Act compliant

Assessment-to-interview face matching

A proxy can take the clinical assessment while a different person attends the interview — and your ATS won't know the difference. FRACIFY matches the face from the assessment session against the interview feed and the identity document in your system.

  • Face matching across assessment, interview, and identity documents
  • Audio-visual latency analysis for off-screen impersonator detection
  • Keystroke and behavioral biometrics correlation across sessions
  • Real-time alerting for identity mismatches between tools
  • Unified confidence score delivered to ATS dashboard
