Augmenting clinicians with AI at the bedside

Healthcare systems are under unprecedented strain. FRACIFY deploys specialized AI agents that work alongside medical professionals — handling the data-intensive tasks so clinicians can spend more time with patients. Our agents integrate with existing EHR systems, imaging platforms, and laboratory information systems.

Autonomous diagnostics & triage

Our diagnostic agent network ingests patient data from multiple sources — vitals, lab results, imaging reports, and historical records — to provide real-time triage recommendations. In emergency settings, this reduces time-to-decision from minutes to seconds.

  • Real-time triage prioritization based on acuity scoring
  • Multi-modal diagnostic fusion (imaging, labs, genomics, vitals)
  • Differential diagnosis generation with confidence scoring
  • Integration with PACS, EHR, and LIS systems via HL7/FHIR
  • Continuous monitoring with automated escalation protocols

Medical imaging intelligence

FRACIFY's vision agents analyze X-rays, CT scans, MRIs, and pathology slides with accuracy matching fellowship-trained radiologists. Agents specialize by modality and anatomy, collaborating to provide comprehensive findings.

  • Chest X-ray analysis for pneumonia, nodules, and effusions
  • CT stroke detection with ASPECTS scoring
  • Mammography screening with CAD integration
  • Retinal image analysis for diabetic retinopathy
  • Pathology slide analysis for cancer grading

Drug discovery acceleration

FRACIFY agents simulate molecular interactions, predict drug-target binding, and optimize clinical trial protocols — reducing the typical 10-year drug development cycle by up to 60%.

  • Virtual screening of billions of compounds against protein targets
  • ADMET prediction for early toxicity filtering
  • Clinical trial protocol optimization with patient matching
  • Real-world evidence analysis from electronic health records
  • Literature mining for drug repurposing opportunities

Clinical documentation & operations

Physicians spend nearly 2 hours on documentation for every hour of patient care. FRACIFY's ambient documentation agents automatically generate clinical notes from conversations, reducing burnout and improving billing accuracy.

  • Ambient speech-to-clinical-note generation
  • Automated ICD-10 and CPT coding from natural language
  • Patient portal summary generation in plain language
  • Prior authorization automation with payer-specific rules
  • Operational analytics for bed management and staffing
