Healthcare organizations use separate tools for AI interviews, clinical assessments, video interviews, and credential verification. Each tool works in isolation — so a proxy candidate who clears the assessment with fake credentials won't be flagged when a different person shows up for the video interview. FRACIFY's unified orchestration layer sits across every tool, cross-references identity data at every stage, and delivers one confidence score — zero data stored.
