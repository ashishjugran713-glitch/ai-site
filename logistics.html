Swarms of agents optimizing every link in the chain

Modern supply chains are complex networks of suppliers, manufacturers, warehouses, carriers, and customers. FRACIFY's agent swarms continuously optimize this network — rerouting around disruptions, balancing inventory across nodes, and predicting demand before it materializes.

Supply chain optimization

Our agents model the entire supply chain as a dynamic graph, continuously optimizing procurement, production, and distribution decisions. Each agent manages a specific node or relationship, collaborating to achieve global optimization targets.

  • Multi-echelon inventory optimization with service-level targets
  • Supplier risk scoring with real-time geopolitical and weather monitoring
  • Dynamic sourcing recommendations based on cost, quality, and lead time
  • Production scheduling with constraint-aware optimization
  • Disruption detection and automated contingency planning

Fleet routing & optimization

FRACIFY's routing agents solve the vehicle routing problem at enterprise scale — optimizing thousands of vehicles across millions of stops per day. Agents adapt to traffic, weather, and delivery windows in real-time.

  • Dynamic route optimization with live traffic and weather data
  • Multi-depot, multi-vehicle routing with time windows
  • Electric vehicle fleet management with charging station optimization
  • Real-time rerouting on disruption events
  • Driver behavior analysis for fuel efficiency improvement

Warehouse automation

Warehouses are the beating heart of logistics. FRACIFY agents coordinate robotic fleets, optimize pick paths, and predict maintenance needs — turning warehouses into autonomous fulfillment centers.

  • Autonomous mobile robot (AMR) fleet coordination
  • Dynamic slotting optimization based on item velocity and affinity
  • Pick path optimization for human and robotic pickers
  • Predictive maintenance for conveyor systems and robotics
  • Labor forecasting and shift optimization

Demand forecasting

Accurate demand forecasting is the foundation of efficient logistics. FRACIFY's forecasting agents combine internal sales data with external signals — weather, economic indicators, social trends — to predict demand at SKU-store-day granularity.

  • Hierarchical forecasting at SKU, category, and aggregate levels
  • External signal integration (weather, economic, social media)
  • Promotion response modeling with causal inference
  • New product forecasting with cross-category similarity
  • Automated replenishment with service-level optimization
