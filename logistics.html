High-volume hiring needs automated identity matching across tools

Logistics companies process thousands of applications through separate ATS, assessment platforms, BGV vendors, and identity systems. Candidate data doesn't match across them — so an impersonator who clears one stage under a fake identity can sail through the rest undetected. FRACIFY sits across every tool and automatically matches identity at every stage — zero data stored.

ATS-to-assessment identity cross-reference

High-volume recruitment makes manual identity checks impossible. Impersonation syndicates exploit this by having proxies take assessments for fake candidates in the ATS. FRACIFY cross-references every assessment session against the corresponding ATS profile — flagging identity mismatches automatically at scale.

  • ATS candidate name cross-referenced against assessment session identity
  • Keystroke and device fingerprint correlation across all candidates
  • Automated face matching between assessment and BGV identity documents
  • Multi-location impersonation pattern detection across high-volume pipelines
  • Real-time confidence score delivered to ATS dashboard

BGV and identity reconciliation at scale

Background checks for thousands of candidates produce reports that don't automatically match against assessment data or ATS profiles. FRACIFY reconciles BGV reports, EPFO records, and identity management data against every candidate's assessment and interview history — automatically.

  • Automated UAN/EPFO timeline audit reconciled against ATS employment history
  • Batch identity matching for high-volume recruitment campaigns
  • Real-time confidence score for every candidate across all tools
  • Duplicate and coordinated identity detection across application pools
  • Zero configuration — plug and play with existing ATS

End-to-end identity from ATS to Day One

Bait-and-switch in logistics means impostors gaining access to inventory systems and fleet platforms. FRACIFY matches identity across ATS, assessments, BGV, and identity management — ensuring the person who applied is the person who starts.
