Logistics hiring volumes create blind spots — unified cross-tool verification closes them

Logistics companies process thousands of applications monthly using separate AI interviews, coding assessments, video platforms, and ATS. These tools don't share data — so an impersonator who clears the assessment with a proxy can pass the video interview with a different stand-in. FRACIFY's unified orchestration layer sits across every tool, cross-references identity data at every stage, and delivers one confidence score for every candidate — zero data stored.
