Banking and fintech hiring requires a unified anti-impersonation layer

Financial institutions use separate tools for AI interviews, coding assessments, video interviews, and ATS. These tools don't share data — so a proxy candidate who passes the assessment with a fake identity won't be flagged when a different face shows up for the video interview. FRACIFY's stateless orchestration layer sits across all your tools, cross-references identity data at every touchpoint, and delivers one unified confidence score — without storing any personal data.

Proxy impersonation detection

Organized syndicates use remote desktop tools and expert proxies to impersonate candidates during technical assessments for banking tech roles. FRACIFY's anti-impersonation engine analyzes keystroke dynamics, mouse movement patterns, and remote desktop signatures to flag impersonators who are not who they claim to be.

  • Real-time keystroke anomaly detection during coding assessments
  • Remote desktop signature recognition for VNC/RDP/TeamViewer impersonation
  • Tab-switching and window-focus monitoring during live tests
  • Cross-candidate IP and device fingerprint correlation for impersonation rings
  • Instant imperonation risk scoring delivered to recruiter dashboard

Identity and credential fraud prevention

Fabricated employment histories and identity impersonation are the most common fraud vectors in finance hiring. FRACIFY's UAN and EPFO timeline audits verify every candidate's employment history against government databases in real-time — flagging impersonation and fabricated identities before offer letters are generated.

  • Instant UAN-based employment timeline verification
  • EPFO contribution matching against claimed employer records
  • Employment gap analysis for fabricated identity detection
  • Automated imperonation reporting with audit trails
  • Zero data stored — fully DPDP Act compliant

Bait-and-switch impersonation protection

The "bait-and-switch" impersonation — where a candidate who passes interviews is replaced by a completely different person on Day One — is a growing threat in finance recruitment. FRACIFY's 3-point facial recognition anchors identity from application through onboarding.

  • Multi-point facial matching across assessment, interview, and Day One
  • Liveness detection to prevent photo and video spoofing
  • Automated identity verification workflow for onboarding teams
  • Real-time alerts for identity mismatches before access is granted
  • Continuous verification across the entire hiring lifecycle
