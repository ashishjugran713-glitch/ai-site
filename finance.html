Banking hires require end-to-end candidate identity matching

Financial institutions use separate tools for ATS, coding assessments, BGV, and identity management. Candidate data doesn't match across these systems — so a proxy candidate who passes the assessment under a fake name won't be flagged when different credentials show up in the BGV report. FRACIFY sits across all your tools and matches identity data at every touchpoint, delivering one unified confidence score — without storing any personal data.

ATS-to-assessment identity matching

Candidates can apply as one person in the ATS and have a proxy take the coding assessment under that name. FRACIFY matches the test-taker's identity — keystroke patterns, device fingerprints, face capture — against the ATS candidate profile, flagging mismatches before the interview stage.

  • ATS candidate profile cross-referenced against assessment session data
  • Keystroke dynamics and device fingerprint correlation
  • Face matching between assessment and interview stages
  • Cross-candidate IP and device clustering for syndicate detection
  • Instant confidence score delivered to recruiter dashboard

BGV and identity data reconciliation

Background verification reports and identity management data often contradict each other — and neither matches what the assessment tool captured. FRACIFY aggregates UAN/EPFO records, document verification results, and assessment data into a single identity profile.

  • Instant UAN-based employment timeline verification
  • EPFO contribution matching against claimed employer records
  • Employment gap analysis for fabricated identity detection
  • Automated imperonation reporting with audit trails
  • Zero data stored — fully DPDP Act compliant

Day-One confidence — matching identity from application to onboarding

The "bait-and-switch" — where a different person shows up on Day One — is fundamentally a data matching failure. FRACIFY's 3-point facial recognition anchors identity from the ATS application through assessment, BGV, and Day One check-in, ensuring it's the same person at every stage.

  • Multi-point identity matching from ATS application through Day One onboarding
  • Liveness detection preventing photo and video spoofing during onboarding
  • Automated identity reconciliation for onboarding teams
  • Real-time alerts for identity mismatches before access is granted
  • Continuous identity verification across the entire hiring lifecycle
