The Problem

Impersonification is a recruitment data problem

You have different tools for different stages — an ATS for hiring flow, assessment vendors for coding tests, BGV vendors for background checks, identity management tools for verification. But candidate data doesn't match across these tools. The ATS says one name, the assessment vendor captured a different face, the BGV report shows different employment history. Recruiters cannot tell if the same candidate has gone through the entire hiring journey. This fragmentation is the root cause of impersonification — and no single tool solves it because no single tool sees the full picture.

ATS and assessment don't talk

The candidate's name in your ATS doesn't match the face that took the coding assessment. Without a unified layer, there's no way to know if the person who passed the test is the same person in your pipeline.

BGV and identity tools are siloed

Your BGV vendor checks employment history. Your identity management tool verifies documents. Neither cross-references with assessment data or interview recordings. Recruiters manually juggle reports from 4+ tools trying to connect the dots.

No end-to-end candidate journey view

Without a layer that matches candidate identity across ATS, assessments, BGV, and identity management, recruiters can never be confident the same person started and finished the hiring process. This is the root cause of impersonification — and it's a data matching problem, not a tool problem.

The Solution

We match candidate identity across ATS, assessments, BGV & identity tools

FRACIFY is a stateless orchestration layer that sits on top of your ATS, assessment platforms, background verification vendors, and identity management systems. It automatically aggregates candidate data from every tool, cross-references identity signals across touchpoints, and tells your recruiter if it's the same person throughout the entire hiring journey — delivered as a single confidence score.

ATS Integration

Connects to your ATS and cross-references every candidate's identity data against assessment results, BGV reports, and identity documents — so recruiters see a unified profile instead of juggling tabs.

Assessment Matching

Pulls data from your coding assessment vendor and matches the test-taker's identity against the ATS candidate profile. Flags mismatches between who applied, who took the test, and who shows up for interviews.

BGV & Identity Cross-Reference

Aggregates background verification reports and identity management data — EPFO records, document verification, facial matching — and cross-references them against assessment and interview data for end-to-end identity assurance.

Confidence Score

All cross-referenced data from ATS, assessments, BGV, and identity tools is distilled into one unified confidence score. One number tells your recruiter: this is the same candidate from application to Day One.

How it Works

From disconnected tools to unified candidate identity in four steps

01

Connect

FRACIFY sits on top of your ATS, assessment platforms, BGV vendors, and identity management tools. One integration connects all of them into a single identity orchestration layer.

02

Aggregate

Automatically collect candidate data from every tool — ATS profiles, assessment session logs, BGV reports, identity verification results — into one intelligence pipeline.

Cross-Reference
