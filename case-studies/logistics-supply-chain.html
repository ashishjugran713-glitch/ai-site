The challenge

GlobalLink Logistics operates a complex global supply chain spanning 47 warehouses across 12 countries, managing over 500,000 SKUs and 15,000 daily shipments. Their legacy optimization system was struggling with increasing volatility — port congestion, fuel price fluctuations, and shifting demand patterns were causing service failures and cost overruns.

On-time delivery rates had slipped to 78%. Inventory carrying costs were eating into margins at $230M annually. The operations team relied on spreadsheets and intuition for critical decisions, with no unified view of the end-to-end supply chain. Each disruption required hours of manual analysis to determine the optimal response.

"Our supply chain had become too complex for traditional optimization tools. Every decision involved trade-offs between cost, speed, and reliability, and by the time we analyzed the options, the window for action had already closed. We needed an AI that could see the whole picture and act instantly."

— Maria Vasquez
SVP of Global Operations, GlobalLink Logistics

The solution

FRACIFY deployed a swarm of 16 specialized agents that model the entire supply chain as a dynamic graph. Each agent manages a specific domain — procurement, inventory, routing, warehousing, or demand forecasting — and they continuously negotiate to find globally optimal solutions.

Agent architecture

Agent 01

Demand forecaster

Predicts demand at SKU-location-day granularity using transformer models trained on 5 years of historical data, weather patterns, and economic indicators.

Agent 02

Inventory optimizer

Balances inventory across 47 hubs using multi-echelon optimization with service-level targets, reducing stockouts while minimizing carrying costs.

Agent 03

Route planner

Solves the vehicle routing problem across 15,000 daily shipments with live traffic, weather, and delivery window constraints.

Agent 04

Disruption handler

Monitors 200+ external data feeds for disruptions — port closures, weather events, geopolitical risks — and triggers automated contingency plans.

Implementation

Deployment was rolled out across all 47 hubs over 20 weeks using a region-by-region approach. Each region's agent swarm was trained on local data for 4 weeks before going live, with a 2-week shadow mode period for validation.

  • Digital twin simulation environment for scenario testing before live deployment
  • Real-time IoT integration with 12,000+ GPS trackers, warehouse sensors, and port APIs
  • Parallel run mode for 4 weeks per region, comparing agent recommendations against human decisions
  • Automated playbook generation for common disruption scenarios
  • Continuous reinforcement learning from outcome data and operator feedback

Results

Within the first 6 months of full deployment, FRACIFY's agent swarm transformed GlobalLink's supply chain operations:

  • 32% reduction in total logistics costs — saving $47M annually
  • 18% improvement in on-time delivery rates — from 78% to 96%
  • 96% inventory accuracy — up from 72% — reducing stockouts by 64%
  • 73% faster disruption response — from 4 hours to under 15 minutes
  • 28% reduction in empty miles through optimized backhaul routing
  • 15% decrease in carbon emissions through route consolidation and modal shifts
