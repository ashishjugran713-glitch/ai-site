About GlobalLink Logistics

GlobalLink Logistics operates a complex global supply chain spanning 47 warehouses across 12 countries, managing over 500,000 SKUs and 15,000 daily shipments. They serve 2,400+ enterprise customers across manufacturing, retail, and healthcare verticals.

The challenge

A supply chain too complex for traditional tools

GlobalLink's legacy optimization system was struggling with increasing volatility — port congestion, fuel price fluctuations, and shifting demand patterns. On-time delivery rates had slipped to 78%. Inventory carrying costs consumed $230M annually. The operations team relied on spreadsheets for critical decisions, with each disruption requiring hours of manual analysis.

Top challenges

  • On-time delivery rates had fallen to 78% across 47 hubs
  • $230M annual inventory carrying costs eroding margins
  • Hours of manual analysis required per supply chain disruption
  • No unified view of end-to-end supply chain operations

Skills deployed

  • Multi-echelon inventory optimization across 47 hubs
  • Real-time VRP solver for 15,000 daily shipments
  • Digital twin simulation for scenario testing
  • Weather and geo-risk disruption monitoring
The solution

16 agents continuously optimizing every link

FRACIFY deployed a swarm of 16 specialized agents that model the entire supply chain as a dynamic graph. Each agent manages a specific domain — demand forecasting, inventory optimization, route planning, disruption handling — and they continuously negotiate to find globally optimal solutions across cost, speed, and reliability trade-offs.
