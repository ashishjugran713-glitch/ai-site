About ShopMax Retail

ShopMax Retail operates 500 stores across North America with 12 million active customers. The chain sells apparel, electronics, home goods, and beauty products through physical stores, e-commerce, and a mobile app serving 2.8M monthly active users.

The challenge

One-size-fits-none personalization

ShopMax's legacy personalization engine was generating only 8% click-through rates on product recommendations. Cross-sell was limited to basic "customers also bought" rules. With 12M customers shopping across 500 stores, e-commerce, and mobile, ShopMax was losing market share to digitally-native competitors who delivered hyper-personalized experiences at scale.

Top challenges

  • Only 8% click-through rate on generic product recommendations
  • No unified customer profile across online and in-store channels
  • Legacy ML infrastructure couldn't process real-time signals
  • Losing market share to Amazon and other digital competitors

Skills deployed

  • 6-agent personalization swarm with real-time customer 360
  • Product graph embeddings for intelligent cross-sell
  • Multi-armed bandit engine for offer optimization
  • Cross-channel session unification (web + store + app)
The solution

6 agents delivering individualized experiences

FRACIFY deployed a multi-agent personalization swarm with 6 specialized agents that collaborate to deliver a unified, real-time personalized experience across every touchpoint — website, mobile app, email, in-store kiosks, and point-of-sale. The agents build a real-time customer 360 profile and use multi-armed bandit algorithms to optimize every recommendation.
