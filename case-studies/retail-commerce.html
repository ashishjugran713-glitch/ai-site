The challenge

ShopMax Retail operates 500 stores across North America with 12 million active customers. Despite investing heavily in e-commerce and loyalty programs, their personalization engine was underperforming — generic product recommendations were generating only 8% click-through rates, and cross-sell was limited to simple "customers also bought" rules.

The retail chain was losing market share to Amazon and other digitally-native competitors who offered hyper-personalized experiences. ShopMax needed to deliver individualized experiences across both online and in-store channels, but their legacy ML infrastructure couldn't process real-time signals or unify online-offline customer journeys.

The solution

FRACIFY deployed a multi-agent personalization swarm with 6 specialized agents that collaborate to deliver a unified, real-time personalized experience across every touchpoint.

Technology stack

FRACIFY Orchestrator Real-time customer 360 Product graph embeddings Multi-armed bandit engine Cross-channel session unification In-store beacon integration

Results

  • 28% increase in average order value through intelligent cross-sell and bundling
  • 34% uplift in online conversion rates with real-time personalized recommendations
  • 3.2x improvement in email campaign ROI through agent-optimized send times and content
  • 22% increase in in-store basket size via personalized mobile offers at point-of-sale
  • $156M annual revenue impact across online and in-store channels
