Stopping the remote developer bait-and-switch

A rapidly scaling digital health platform deployed FRACIFY's talent integrity layer to neutralize a sophisticated proxy interview ring that was infiltrating their full-stack developer hiring pipeline.

Client Profile

A rapidly scaling digital health and fitness platform hiring 40+ full-stack developers per quarter to expand their mobile infrastructure. The company needed to accelerate engineering hiring velocity without compromising on talent integrity or security.

Hiring Velocity
40+ devs/quarter
Fraud Vector
Proxy interview ring
The problem

Impeccable resumes, catastrophic code

The client's talent acquisition team fell victim to an industrialized proxy interview ring. Candidates with impeccable, industry-standard resumes cleared the initial automated screen and a live technical coding round over Zoom. However, engineering managers noticed a catastrophic drop in code quality within two weeks of onboarding. Sophisticated bad actors were using elite technical proxies to take the video interviews, while completely different, unqualified individuals reported to work on Day One.

Key challenges

  • Industrialized proxy interview ring passing all screening stages
  • Catastrophic code quality drop within two weeks of onboarding
  • No identity continuity check between interview and Day One
  • Engineering velocity derailed by bad hires requiring replacement

Capabilities deployed

  • 3-point facial recognition continuity anchoring
  • Real-time video liveness verification integration
  • Stateless ATS middleware for unified screening
  • Automated identity alert at onboarding mismatch
The solution

Identity continuity from application to onboarding

The company deployed our battle-tested talent integrity orchestration layer. Our stateless middleware instantly unified their existing Applicant Tracking System (ATS) with real-time video liveness tools. By automating a 3-point facial recognition continuity check, the system anchored the candidate's identity across the entire pipeline. The moment an onboarding video stream failed to match the original interview baseline, the system triggered an immediate alert — neutralizing bait-and-switch fraud before corporate system access was ever granted.
