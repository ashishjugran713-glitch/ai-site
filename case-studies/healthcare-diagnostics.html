The challenge

Critical bottlenecks in emergency care

Emergency departments across MediCare's network were critically strained. Average door-to-triage time was 47 minutes, and door-to-diagnosis averaged 3.2 hours for non-critical cases. Stroke patients faced a dangerous 78-minute wait for CT interpretation. With a shortage of radiologists and emergency physicians, diagnostic errors occurred at a rate of 8.2 per 1,000 cases.

The solution

Collaborative AI agents at the bedside

FRACIFY deployed 8 specialized diagnostic agents that work in parallel, ingesting patient data the moment it becomes available — vitals, lab results, imaging, and historical records. The triage prioritizer assigns acuity scores in 1.2 seconds. The imaging analyst flags critical findings on CT and X-ray in real-time. The differential generator fuses all outputs into a ranked diagnosis with confidence scores and recommended next steps.
