The challenge

MediCare Health operates a network of 12 hospitals across 5 countries, handling over 2.1 million patient visits annually. Their emergency departments were experiencing critical bottlenecks — average door-to-triage time was 47 minutes, and door-to-diagnosis averaged 3.2 hours for non-critical cases. Stroke patients faced an average of 78 minutes to CT interpretation.

With a shortage of radiologists and emergency physicians, the clinical team was stretched thin. Diagnostic errors in the emergency department were occurring at a rate of 8.2 per 1,000 cases, and the hospital network was facing rising malpractice premiums and declining patient satisfaction scores.

"In emergency medicine, minutes matter. We knew AI could help, but we needed a system that didn't just flag anomalies — it needed to think like a seasoned clinician, weighing symptoms, history, and diagnostics together in seconds."

— Dr. Amara Obi
Chief Medical Information Officer, MediCare Health

The solution

FRACIFY deployed a collaborative agent swarm consisting of 8 specialized diagnostic agents, each trained on specific clinical domains. The agents work in parallel, ingesting patient data the moment it becomes available — vitals, lab results, imaging, and historical records — and collaboratively generating a prioritized differential diagnosis.

Agent architecture

Agent 01

Triage prioritizer

Evaluates presenting complaints, vital signs, and risk factors to assign an acuity score within 1.2 seconds of patient registration.

Agent 02

Imaging analyst

Analyzes CT, X-ray, and MRI scans in real-time, flagging critical findings like intracranial hemorrhage, pulmonary embolism, and fractures.

Agent 03

Lab interpreter

Correlates laboratory results with clinical presentation, identifying patterns indicative of sepsis, MI, DKA, and other time-sensitive conditions.

Agent 04

Differential generator

Fuses outputs from all agents with the patient's EHR to generate a ranked differential diagnosis with confidence scores and recommended next steps.

Implementation

The deployment was executed across all 12 hospitals over 18 weeks, following strict HIPAA and GDPR compliance protocols. The system was deployed in a parallel assistive mode for 8 weeks, where agent recommendations were compared against physician decisions before going live.

  • HIPAA-compliant architecture with end-to-end encryption and audit logging
  • Integration with existing EHR (Epic), PACS, and LIS via FHIR R4 and HL7 v2
  • Shadow mode deployment for 8 weeks with continuous validation against physician decisions
  • Automated escalation protocol for critical findings — alerts radiologist within 30 seconds
  • Continuous learning from confirmed diagnoses and peer review outcomes

Results

Within 6 months of full deployment, FRACIFY's diagnostic agents transformed clinical operations across the MediCare Health network:

  • 89% reduction in triage time — from 47 minutes to under 5 minutes
  • 96.3% diagnostic accuracy — surpassing the pre-deployment physician accuracy of 92.1%
  • 62% faster stroke diagnosis — door-to-CT-interpretation reduced from 78 to 29 minutes
  • 41% decrease in malpractice incidents related to diagnostic delay
  • 27% improvement in patient satisfaction scores for emergency department visits
  • $8.2M annual savings from reduced length of stay and improved clinical efficiency
