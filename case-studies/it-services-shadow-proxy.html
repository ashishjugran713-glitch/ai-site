Stopping the automated shadow-proxy coding test syndicate

Client Profile

An elite global IT services and enterprise consulting firm conducting high-volume recruitment of cloud architects and software engineers for international client deployments. The company used industry-standard technical testing platforms but remained vulnerable to sophisticated shadow-proxy attacks.

Hiring Volume
3,000+ assessments/quarter
Fraud Vector
Remote-desktop shadow proxy
The problem

Cameras on, accomplices coding off-screen

Despite using industry-standard technical testing platforms, the client was plagued by "shadow-proxying." Candidates kept their cameras active during code assessments but utilized dual-monitor arrays or remote-desktop software (like AnyDesk) to let an off-screen accomplice write the code. Recruiters were buried under a mountain of fragmented data reports from different screening tools, failing to tie these subtle technical anomalies together until it was too late.

Key challenges

  • Dual-monitor and remote-desktop shadow-proxying undetected by platform tools
  • Fragmented data across screening tools with no unified view
  • Recruiters drowning in reports instead of assessing candidates
  • Fraud detected too late — after offers extended to unqualified candidates

Capabilities deployed

  • Keystroke velocity mapping for real-time anomaly detection
  • Dual-cursor and remote-desktop signature recognition
  • Audio-visual latency tracking during live assessments
  • Unified risk score delivered directly in ATS dashboard
The solution

A unified intelligence shield for high-volume technical hiring

The client implemented our veteran-grade orchestration middleware to serve as a unified intelligence shield. Instead of forcing recruiters to open multiple browser tabs to manually review proctoring logs, our stateless pipeline aggregated live webhook data from their assessment engines. The system instantly combined keystroke velocity mapping, dual-cursor detection, and audio-visual latency tracking into a single, definitive risk score delivered directly inside their primary ATS dashboard. This completely eliminated manual verification lag and stopped tech proxy fraud dead in its tracks.
