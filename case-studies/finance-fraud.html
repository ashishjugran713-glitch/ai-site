About NexaPay

NexaPay is a leading digital banking platform operating across 12 countries, processing over 50 million transactions daily. The platform serves 24 million active users and handles $2.8B in transaction volume annually.

The challenge

Drowning in alerts, missing real threats

NexaPay's rule-based fraud detection system was generating an unmanageable volume of false positives — over 18% of legitimate transactions were flagged for manual review. A team of 200+ analysts was spending 80% of their time investigating false alarms while real fraud slipped through. The bank was losing $12M per quarter in abandoned transactions due to false declines, with actual fraud losses at $85M annually.

Top challenges

  • 18% false positive rate overwhelming 200+ fraud analysts
  • Legacy ML models required weeks to retrain — too slow for evolving fraud patterns
  • $85M annual fraud losses with $12M quarterly revenue loss from false declines
  • No cross-channel fraud correlation across web, mobile, and API transactions

Skills deployed

  • Multi-agent AI fraud detection with 12 specialized agents
  • Real-time graph neural networks for synthetic identity detection
  • Explainable AI with full audit trails for regulatory compliance
  • Federated learning for cross-border fraud pattern recognition
The solution

Collaborative AI agents that think like fraud investigators

FRACIFY deployed a swarm of 12 specialized AI agents, each focused on a specific fraud vector. The agents operate in a collaborative architecture, sharing signals and building a unified risk profile for every transaction in under 12 milliseconds. Each agent specializes — transaction profiling, identity verification, network analysis, compliance monitoring — and they continuously learn from each other's findings.
