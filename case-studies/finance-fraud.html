The challenge

NexaPay, a digital banking platform operating across 12 countries, was processing over 50 million transactions daily. Their existing rule-based fraud detection system was generating an unmanageable volume of false positives — over 18% of legitimate transactions were being flagged for manual review, causing friction for customers and overwhelming their fraud operations team of 200+ analysts.

The bank was losing an estimated $12M per quarter in abandoned transactions due to false declines, while actual fraud losses were still averaging $85M annually. Legacy ML models required weeks to retrain and couldn't adapt quickly enough to emerging fraud patterns.

"We were drowning in alerts. Our analysts spent 80% of their time investigating false positives, and real fraud was slipping through the cracks. We needed a system that could think like a fraud investigator but operate at machine speed."

— Rajesh Mehta
VP of Fraud Operations, NexaPay

The solution

FRACIFY deployed a swarm of 12 specialized AI agents, each focused on a specific fraud vector. The agents operate in a collaborative architecture, sharing signals and building a unified risk profile for every transaction in under 12 milliseconds.

Agent architecture

Agent 01

Transaction profiler

Scores each transaction against 200+ behavioral signals in real-time, including velocity, amount anomalies, and merchant category patterns.

Agent 02

Identity verifier

Cross-references device fingerprint, biometric data, IP geolocation, and historical login patterns to detect account takeover attempts.

Agent 03

Network analyst

Builds real-time graph networks of connected accounts, devices, and merchants to identify synthetic identity rings and organized fraud rings.

Agent 04

Compliance monitor

Flag transactions against global sanctions lists, PEP databases, and regulatory thresholds across all 12 operating jurisdictions.

Implementation

The deployment followed a phased approach over 14 weeks. The first phase deployed the transaction profiler agent alongside the existing rule engine in shadow mode. Over weeks 3-6, additional agents were brought online with real-time feedback loops. By week 10, the full agent swarm was processing 100% of transactions.

  • Zero-downtime deployment with parallel run mode for 6 weeks
  • Automated A/B testing framework comparing agent decisions against human analysts
  • Continuous learning pipeline with daily model updates from confirmed fraud cases
  • Full audit trail with explainable AI reasoning for every declined transaction
  • Regulatory compliance across 12 countries including PSD2, PCI DSS, and local banking regulations

Results

Within the first quarter of full production deployment, FRACIFY's agent swarm transformed NexaPay's fraud operations:

  • 94% reduction in false positive rate — from 18% to just 1.1% of transactions flagged
  • 99.97% detection accuracy for confirmed fraudulent transactions
  • $340M annually in prevented fraud losses
  • 89% reduction in manual review workload — team rebalanced from investigation to strategy
  • 2.3x improvement in real-time decision speed at 12ms median latency
  • 42% decrease in customer complaints related to false declines
