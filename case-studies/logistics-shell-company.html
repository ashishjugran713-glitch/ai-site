Client Profile

A high-velocity logistics and supply-chain network onboarding senior data analysts and product managers to handle massive transaction volumes. The company trusted traditional background verification vendors to validate candidate experience, but was unknowingly bleeding lakhs on bad hires.

The problem

Clean paper trails, fabricated careers

Recruiters were hit by a wave of hyper-convincing resume fraud. Candidates submitted clean paper trails and experience letters from companies fully registered with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA). Traditional background verification (BGV) vendors took 14 days to return standard checklists, often marking them as "verified" simply because a fake HR manager answered a coordinated phone call. The company was bleeding lakhs on bad hires who completely lacked the required senior infrastructure capabilities.

Key challenges

  • Shell companies with legitimate MCA registration used as fake employers
  • 14-day BGV turnaround too slow to catch fabricated tenures
  • Coordinated phone rings where fake HR managers verified each other
  • ₹45 Lakhs+ lost on bad hires lacking required capabilities

Capabilities deployed

  • Real-time UAN and EPFO timeline audit against government records
  • Automated AI experience gap analysis
  • Shell company domain detection via corporate registry cross-reference
  • Stateless router — zero candidate data stored
The solution

Real-time government audit vs. slow manual checkmarks

We pioneered a real-time validation pipeline that bypassed slow, manual checkmarks entirely. The client integrated our stateless router directly into their core recruitment workflow. Our system executed an instant, automated UAN and EPFO timeline audit the moment a candidate was shortlisted. By cross-referencing real-time government contribution dates against stated resume parameters, our AI engine instantly flagged artificial experience gaps and shell company domains, saving the client over ₹45 Lakhs in misallocated engineering salaries.
